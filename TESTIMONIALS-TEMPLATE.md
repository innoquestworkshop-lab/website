## Audience options
- `student` — a student who attended a program
- `parent` — a parent of a participant
- `school` — a teacher, principal, or school admin
- `corporate` — a CSR director, HR lead, or company rep

## Program slugs (for programSlug field)
Leave blank if not applicable, otherwise use one of:
- `incsii-playground`
- `price-war`
- `break-the-market`
- `entre`
- `epac`
